✦ Our Story

Where Heritage
Meets Grace

At Sohni Sajri, we celebrate timeless Punjabi elegance with a modern touch. Rooted in rich traditions and inspired by the vibrant spirit of Punjab, our brand brings you handpicked unstitched suits that reflect grace, culture, and craftsmanship.

We believe every woman deserves to feel Sohni (beautiful) and Sajri (adorned) — from casual classics to festive finery, crafted with love for women who cherish tradition.
